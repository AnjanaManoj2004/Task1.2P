Here are some nutritional tips for you to follow:


1. Adopt Whole Foods: Always choose unprocessed foods over processed ones as they contain less harmful substances in them.

2. Prioritise Portion Control: Watch your portions to avoid overeating. For natural portion control, use smaller dishes, plates, and utensils.

3. Balance: Aim for a balanced diet as it is essential to have a balanced diet inorder to provide our body with the nutrients it needs to function.

4. Include Lean Proteins: Always include lean protein in your diet as protein is essential for the functioning of our body.
